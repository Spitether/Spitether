Store Policies

Shipping

Processing time is 2–4 business days. Because items are pre-loved and one-of-a-kind, each piece is inspected and packaged with care. Shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination. Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email.

Returns

Due to the nature of pre-loved and one-of-a-kind items, all sales are final. Please review measurements, photos, and descriptions carefully before purchasing. If your item arrives damaged or significantly different from the description, contact me within 48 hours of delivery with video proof of unboxing the item, so I can help resolve the issue.

Terms

All items are sold as-is and may show signs of wear consistent with their age. Colors may vary slightly due to lighting and screen differences. By purchasing from this shop, you agree to the policies listed above and acknowledge that each item is unique and may have minor imperfections.
